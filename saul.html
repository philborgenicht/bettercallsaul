Saul Goodman, Attorney at Law, Founder and Managing Partner.

Despite his stellar track record, Saul Goodman did not know the prestige to which would rise in his formative years, or even his early adult years. Saul worked many menial, dead end jobs after high school, and eventually found himself working as a mail clerk in the office of "Hamlin, Hamlin, and McGill, where his brother, Chuck, was one of the founding partners. Saul was so motivated by seeing the work ethic of the attorneys around him, that he pusehd himself to take night classes, get his degree, and eventually pass the bar exam. Saul attended the University of American Samoa, where he studied constitutional Law, and later brushed up on his criminal law and criminal justice at Albuquerque Community College, to be able to help the wrongly accused. For severeal year, he actually practiced under his birth name, James "jimmy" McGill, but later changed his practicing name to "Saul Goodman", a play on words of "it's all good, man".
