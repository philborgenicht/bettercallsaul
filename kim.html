Kim Wexler, Attorney at Law, Managing Partner.

Kim Wexler grew up in a small town on the Kansas-Nebraska border. Always a top performer in high school and a self described perfectionist and over achiever, she knew that she wanted more than what her hometown had to offer. She moved to Albuquerque to attend University, studying law, and shortly after graduation, secured a career at the offices of Hamlin, Hamlin, and McGill. It was there where she would soon meet Saul, and push him to discover his true potential. Little did they know that they would one day become managing partners of their own firm, but here they are, running one of the most successful branches in the state.
